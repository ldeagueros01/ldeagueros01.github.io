Membership

Boxing is a sport that encourages discipline while strengthening mental and physical development. Groves Boxing & Fitness is a gym that fosters these values and encourages individuals of all ages to embrace the "sweet science" of boxing. The goal of Groves Boxing & Fitness is to provide members with the skills of a professional boxer while focusing on the importance and benefits of fitness. Since professional and amateur boxers train at Groves Boxing & Fitness, non-competition members have access to the same high-level training. With fitness being the primary focus for most members, Groves Boxing and Fitness are designed to offer a flexible training schedule, deliver workouts that focus on developing your strength, endurance and agility, while providing an environment that promotes healthy living.

Boxing 101

Boxing 101- is geared mainly toward those who haven’t exercised in a while and want to ease into a workout program, not dive right in. It’s especially good for people who need more time to see if boxing is for them, or anyone who feels the need to “get in shape” before they join a gym. The Intro class will get you physically and mentally ready to start our other boxing programs. ...Customized one-hour boxing lessons with world-class instructors are available for men and women all ages, and levels, from novices to seasoned experts. Learn new boxing skills and techniques, improve endurance, strength and quickness, and achieve your fitness goals. Learn the art and self defense of boxing, while getting into great shape!

Kids Class

Kid’s Boxing classes are designed for boys and girls under the age of 18. We train with jump ropes, heavy bags, speed bags and focus pads, and also utilize reflex drills, hand-eye coordination exercises, footwork drills, and shadow boxing. All of the kids will learn the art and self-defense of boxing, while getting into great shape!

Advance Class

Advanced/competition instruction is designed for those that want to participate in regular sparring and/or compete. Advanced techniques are practiced. Contact in every class. All skills classes preceding the instruction section refine the attributes essential for a boxer including coordination, speed, stamina, strength, movement, and offensive techniques. A high level of independence and skill refinement is expected at this level.
